Homework Questions

a. Place an image in your document. Do not specify width and height. What happens?

The image is displayed at it's maximum size that fits within the window, and allows you to click it to zoom to fullsize (in Firefox and Chrome). The page also takes longer to render because the size of the image isn't specified and needs to be computed.

b. Place the same image in your document but this time specify a width and height that is smaller than the image. What difference do you see?

Specifying the size allows the page to be drawn faster, but the full resolution is still present, compressed (or expanded) into the size specified.
